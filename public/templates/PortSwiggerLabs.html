Portswigger is a fantastic resource for learning about web application security. They offer a range of free labs that are designed to help people understand common vulnerabilities and how to exploit them. These labs cover a wide range of topics, from basic SQL injection to advanced business logic vulnerabilities.

Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced security researcher, Portswigger's labs offer something for everyone. And because they're free, you can learn at your own pace without worrying about the cost. Best of all, the labs are designed to be fun and engaging, so you'll never get bored as you work your way through the exercises.

Here, you will find solutions to these labs. My solutions are written as if it was my first time solving them, meant to show you the thought process behind ethical hacking and pentesting. I provide step-by-step explanation, detailing my thinking at each stage of the process.

These solutions are categorized based on difficulty level, with separate pages for Apprentice, Practioner, and Expert challenges.
